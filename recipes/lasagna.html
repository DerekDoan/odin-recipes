Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find this recipe easily customisable to suit your dietary needs.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato base
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover in foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
